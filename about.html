eweeg

Eweego was born from a childs question: why cant we use a transit card anywhere in the world, just like a credit card?

Technological paradigm
Eweego is a virtual global mobility platform that enables seamless user access to diverse transport services, ranging from public transit to airlines and parking providers, without requiring system-level integration from operators. Instead of directly connecting different providers' systems, Eweego intelligently emulates their ticketing and service logic within a unified platform.
Acting as a smart abstraction layer, it simulates interactions, enforces access rules, and manages fare and usage conditions independently for each provider. This allows users to pay and receive valid travel authorizations through a single account, enabling smooth, cross-provider mobility without the need for API standardization or IT systems interconnection.
